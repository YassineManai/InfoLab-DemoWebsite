Who Are We ?

The INFO-LAB Club , Founded in May 2022, INFO-LAB ESSECT is a University club forESSECT’s students passionate about the world of IT and computing..

We encourage students to develop their interests by offering avariety of extracurricular activities. The principle of Info-Lab is that students grouped in teams carry out development projects and in web design, graphic design, create logos, adapt to use design software & platforms (Illustrator, Canva, Figma ...) for themselves and for the community, we will always have a fun and stimulating activity for you to do! Through this experience, students develop skills that are in high demand in the digital market while improving their group work skills by connecting with others who have similar interests. But what if you wanted to take your passion for one of these interests to the next level? What if you could develop your own websites that could be later monetized? If those are your interests, then the Essect’developers is for you.

What We Do ?

Our primary objective is to enhance the skills of our members by a variety of extracurricular activities. We allow to come together and discuss their passions and ideas. The collaborative approach helps students develop better products through the course of the club, and participation in various training courses offered by Info-Lab are an impressive addition to their college application portfolios.
